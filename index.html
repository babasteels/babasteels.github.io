About

Welcome to Baba Steels! We are one of the leading retail showroom for home appliances in Coimbatore.

Babasteels was started by people with 15 years of experience in whole sale and retail distribution of home appliances.

Our mission is to provide the best quality products at competitive price.

We served over 5000 happy customers in the first 100 days of opening and continue growing. We value the customer and do everything possible to bring high quality products at competitive price.

For any sales enquiries or feedback you can write to contact@babasteels.in
